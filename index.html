Geo-Mapping: Earthquakes

Details

In this assignment, data was obtained from the United States Geological Survey (USGS) website. On the right are earthquakes mapped using leaflet for the past seven days. Some findings from this information were the following:

  • There are a large number occurence of earthquakes in California, but as you navigate towards Central and South America, the occurences decreased significantly.

  • There were no earthquakes recorded in Africa.

  • There are various earthquakes recorded along Indonesia. Most of them varying between 5-6 on the Magnitude Scale.
  • There were a large number of earthquakes recorded along the coastline of Alaska.
